AppliedMicro

AppliedMicro
Type Public
Founded 1979
Headquarters Santa Clara, California
Website https://www.apm.com/

AppliedMicro (APM or AMCC), formerly Applied Micro Circuits Corporation, is an American fabless semiconductor company that designs ARM and Power-based high-speed connectivity and high performance embedded microprocessors for network infrastructure, storage, and servers. In November of 2016, AppliedMicro was acquired by MACOM.
