Dimensity 1000 is a high-performance mobile ARM 5G SoC designed by MediaTek serving as the company's 2020 flagship processor. The Dimensity 1000 is fabricated on TSMC 7 nm process and integrates four high-performance Cortex-A77 big cores operating at 2.6 GHz along with four low-power Cortex-A55 little cores operating at 2 GHz. This chip features a Mali-G77 MP9 GPU operating at ? MHz and supports up to 16 GiB of quad-channel LPDDR4X-3733.

The 1000L is a lower-performance version of this chip. The 1000+ is a higher-performance version of the chip, first appearing in the Vivo iQOO Z1 in May 2020.

Neural processor[edit]

The Dimensity 1000 integrates MediaTek's AI Processing Unit (APU) 3.0 with six cores (2 big, 3 small, and 1 tiny) capable of 4.5 TOPS.
