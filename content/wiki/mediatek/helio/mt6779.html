Helio P90 (MT6779) is a 64-bit ARM LTE SoC introduced by MediaTek in late 2018. Fabricated on TSMC's 12 nm process, the chip incorporates eight cores - six Cortex-A55 little cores operating at up to 2 GHz and two Cortex-A75 cores operating at up to 2.2 Ghz. The P90 incorporates a PowerVR GM 9446 GPU operating at up to 970 MHz and an LTE modem supporting Cat-13 150 Mbps upload and Cat-12 600 Mbps download. This chip supports up to 8 GiB of dual-channel LPDDR4X-3732 memory.
