    Property:l1i$ size
    1766
    U

    This is a quantity property representing the size of the L1i$ of a microprocessor. This property adheres to the WikiChip:data size units policy.

    Units:

    • 1 KiB,kibibyte,kibibytes
    • 1,024 B,byte,bytes
    • 0.0009765625 MiB,mebibyte,mebibytes
