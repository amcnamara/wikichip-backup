Mercury Lake is the name of the core for Intel's generation of system on chip serving as a successor to Gemini Lake. These chips primarily targeted towards entry level 2-in-1 devices, low-cost laptops and desktops, and All-in-One PCs as well as IoTs. Mercury Lake chips are expected to be manufactured on a 10 nm process.
