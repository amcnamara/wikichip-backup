Iris Plus Graphics 650 is a family of Gen 9.5 integrated graphics processors designed by Intel for their Kaby Lake-based microprocessors. The Iris Plus 650 are the high-end (GT3e) IGP offered by Intel with 48 execution units and 64 MiB of dedicated side cache. Iris Plus 650 are found in selected high-end Kaby Lake U models.

Features[edit]

Main article: Kaby Lake §Graphics

All models support the following features:

  • Support for up to three independent displays via HDMI 1.4, DisplayPort (DP) 1.2, an Embedded DisplayPort (eDP) 1.4 interfaces.
  • Quick Sync Video
  • Clear Video
  • Clear Video HD
  • InTru 3D
