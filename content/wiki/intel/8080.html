8080 was an 8-bit microprocessor introduced by Intel in 1974. This MPU was part of the MCS-80 family.

Die Shot[edit]

  • 6 µm process
  • nMOS transistors
  • 4,500 transistors
  • 0.164" x 0.19"
  • 20.1 mm² die size
