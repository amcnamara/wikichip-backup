Gen11 (Generation 11) is the microarchitecture for Intel's graphics processing unit utilized by Icelake-based microprocessors. Gen11 is the successor to Gen10 used by Cannon Lake.

intel vaapi release gen11 details: media_driver/linux/gen11/ddi/media_sysinfo_g11.cpp
