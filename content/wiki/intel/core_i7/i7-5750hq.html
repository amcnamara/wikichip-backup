The Intel Core i7-5850EQ is a quad core 64-bit microprocessor introduced by Intel in 2015. The microprocessor is based on the Broadwell microarchitecture, manufactured using 14nm process and incorporates the Iris Pro Graphics 6200. This microprocessor has configurable TDP-down mode support capable of operating at 600MHz with 37W TDP.
