HD Graphics 510 is an integrated graphics processor introduced by Intel in 2015 for their low-end/low-power Skylake-based microprocessors. This GPU is Skylake's lowest tier GPU incorporating just 12 execution units. The 510 GPU is found in mostly budget processors (i.e., Celeron and Pentium).
