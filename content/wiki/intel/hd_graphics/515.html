HD Graphics 515 is an integrated graphics processor introduced by Intel in 2015 for the extremely low power Skylake-based microprocessors. This GPU is Skylake's mid-tier GPU incorporating 24 execution units. The 515 GPU is found in the extremely low power mobile (Skylake Y) processors.

Features[edit]

Main article: Skylake § Graphics

All models support the following features:

  • Support for up to three independent displays via HDMI 1.4, DisplayPort (DP) 1.2, an Embedded DisplayPort (eDP) 1.3 interfaces.
  • Quick Sync Video
  • Clear Video
  • Clear Video HD
  • InTru 3D
