The Xeon E3-1515M V5 is 64-bit x86 mobile quad-core microprocessor for introduced by Intel in October 2015. This entry-level Skylake-based workstations processor operates at 2.8 GHz with turbo boost of 3.7 GHz. This chip has a TDP of 45 Watts with a configurable TDP down of 35 W. The MPU supports up to 64 GB of dual-channel DDR3/4 and has the Iris Pro Graphics P580 IGP.
