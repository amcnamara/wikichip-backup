The Pentium N3710 is a 64-bit quad-core system on a chip designed by Intel and introduced in early 2015. The N3710 is manufactured in 14 nm process based on the Airmont microarchitecture. This chip operates at 1.6 GHz with turbo mode of up to 2.57 GHz. This SoC incorporates the HD Graphics 405 GPU which has 16 execution units and operates at 400 MHz.
