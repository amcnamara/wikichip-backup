Celeron J3455 is a quad-core 64-bit x86 desktop microprocessor introduced by Intel in 2016. The processor is based on Goldmont microarchitecture and is manufactured on a 14 nm process. The chip operates at 1.5 GHz with burst frequency of 2.3 GHz and has a TDP of 10 W. This MPU incorporates Intel's HD Graphics 500 GPU operating at 250 MHz with a burst frequency of 750 MHz.
