HD Graphics 610 is a family of Gen 9.5 integrated graphics processor designed by Intel for their Kaby Lake-based microprocessors. The 610 are the low-end (GT1) IGP offered by Intel with only 12 execution units and no embedded DRAM. The HD Graphics 610 are only found on Celeron, Pentium, and embedded Core i3 models (Kaby Lake S and Kaby Lake U).

Features[edit]

Main article: Kaby Lake Graphics

All models support the following features:

  • Support for up to three independent displays via HDMI 1.4, DisplayPort (DP) 1.2, an Embedded DisplayPort (eDP) 1.4 interfaces.
  • Quick Sync Video
  • Clear Video
  • Clear Video HD

Some models also support: (Core i3 models)

  • InTru 3D
  • WiDi
