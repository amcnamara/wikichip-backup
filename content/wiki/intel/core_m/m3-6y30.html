The Core M3-6Y30 is an ultra-low power dual-core 64-bit x86 microprocessor introduced by Intel in late 2015. This MPU operates at 900 MHz with a max turbo frequency of 2.2 GHz. This chip, which is manufactured in 14 nm process based on the Skylake microarchitecture and incorporates Intel's HD Graphics 515 Gen9 GPU clocked at 300 MHz with turbo frequency of 850 MHz.
