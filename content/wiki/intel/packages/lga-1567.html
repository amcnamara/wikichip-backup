Socket LGA-1567 - Intel
< intel

Socket LGA-1567 is the socket for FCLGA-8-packaged microprocessor designed by Intel for their high-end enterprise server multiprocessors. This socket was used for chips manufactured based on the Nehalem and Westmere microarchitectures.
