The Intel Core i7-4850HQ is a quad core 64-bit microprocessor designed by Intel in 2013. The microprocessor is based on the Haswell microarchitecture. This MPU includes the Intel Iris Pro 5200 integrated graphic and features a large 128 MB L4$ called Crystal Well.
