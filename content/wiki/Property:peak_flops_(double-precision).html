    Property:peak flops (double-precision)
    20
    U

    This is a quantity representing the peak Double-Precision (64-bit) FLOPS of the system/chip in context.

    Units:

    • 1 FLOPS
    • 0.001 KFLOPS,kiloFLOPS
    • 0.000001 MFLOPS,megaFLOPS
    • 0.000000001 GFLOPS,gigaFLOPS
    • 0.000000000001 TFLOPS,teraFLOPS
    • 0.000000000000001 PFLOPS,petaFLOPS
    • 0.000000000000000001 EFLOPS,exaFLOPS
    • 0.000000000000000000001 ZFLOPS,zetaFLOPS
