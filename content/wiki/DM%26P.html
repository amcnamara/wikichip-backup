DM&P Electronics
Type Private
Founded 1989
Headquarters New Taipei City, Taiwan
Website http://www.dmp.com.tw/

DM&P Electronics, Inc. is a Taiwanese fabless semiconductor company that primarily deals with embedded electronics. DM&P also designs x86 microprocessors for embedded systems.

Chips[edit]

  • Vortex86DX3
  • Vortex86EX
  • Vortex86DX2
  • Vortex86MX+
  • Vortex86DX
  • Vortex86SX
  • M6117D
