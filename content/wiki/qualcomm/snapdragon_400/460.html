Snapdragon 460 is an entry-level 64-bit ARM LTE system on a chip designed by Qualcomm and introduced in early 2020. Fabricated on Samsung's 11nm LPP process, the 460 features four Kryo 240 Silver high-efficiency cores operating at 1.8 GHz along with four high-performance Kryo 240 Gold operating at 1.8 GHz. The Snapdragon 460 integrates the Adreno 610 GPU and features an X11 LTE modem supporting Cat 13 uplink and Cat 12 downlink. This chip supports up to 8 GiB of dual-channel LPDDR4x-3733 memory.
