Snapdragon 720G is a mid-range 64-bit ARM LTE system on a chip designed by Qualcomm and introduced in early 2020. Fabricated on Samsung's 8nm LPP process, the 720G features six Kryo 465 Silver high-efficiency cores operating at 1.8 GHz along with two high-performance Kryo 465 Gold operating at 2.3 GHz. The Snapdragon 720G integrates the Adreno 618 GPU operation at 750 MHz and features an X15 LTE modem supporting Cat 13 uplink and Cat 15 downlink. This chip supports up to 8 GiB of dual-channel LPDDR4X-3733 memory.
