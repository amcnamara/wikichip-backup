Snapdragon 730G is a mid-range 64-bit ARM LTE system on a chip designed by Qualcomm and introduced in early 2019. Fabricated on Samsung's 8nm LPP process, the 730G features six Kryo 470 Silver high-efficiency cores operating at 1.8 GHz along with two high-performance Kryo 470 Gold operating at 2.2 GHz. The Snapdragon 730G integrates the Adreno 618 GPU operation at 575 MHz and features an X15 LTE modem supporting Cat 13 uplink and Cat 15 downlink. This chip supports up to 8 GiB of dual-channel LPDDR4X-3733 memory.

The Snapdragon 730G is a gaming-oriented version of the 730, featuring nearly identical specifications but higher clocked GPU and higher display resolution support.
