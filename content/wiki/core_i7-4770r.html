Core i7-4770R is a quad core 64-bit desktop microprocessor released by Intel in 2013. The microprocessor is based on the Haswell microarchitecture. This MPU includes the Intel Iris Pro 5200 integrated graphic and features the 128 MB L4$ Crystal Well cache.
