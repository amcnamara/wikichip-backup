United Microelectronics Corporation
(Redirected from United Microelectronics Corporation)

UMC
Type Public
Founded 1980
Founder Industrial Technology Research Institute
Headquarters Hsinchu, Taiwan
Website http://www.umc.com/

UMC (United Microelectronics Corporation) is a Taiwan semiconductor company. UMC operates the world's third largest foundry business - after TSMC and GlobalFoundries.
