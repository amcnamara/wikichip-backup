    Property:tdp up
    116
    U

    This is a quantity property representing configurable thermal design power up of the device.

    • 1 W, Watt, Watts
    • 1000 mW, mWatt, milliwatts
    • 0.001341 hp, horsepower
    • 0.001 kW, kilowatts
