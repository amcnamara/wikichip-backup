80186-6/BZC is an 80186-based microprocessor designed by Intel and manufactured by AMD. This model is geared toward aerospace and defense application and thus conforms to MIL-STD-883C. This chip operated at 6 MHz enclosed in PGA packaging.

Graphics[edit]

This chip had no integrated graphics processing unit.

Features[edit]

  • MIL-STD-883C compliant
  • Military temperature range
  • 10 new instructions
  • Two DMA channels
  • Three programmable interrupt timers
  • Local Bus Controller
  • Object code-compatible with all 86/88 software
