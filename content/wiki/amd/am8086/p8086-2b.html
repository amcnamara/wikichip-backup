P8086-2B is a second-sourced 8086 designed by Intel and manufactured by AMD in a 40-pin Plastic DIP. This chip operated at 8 MHz.

Features[edit]

  • Burn-in screening
  • ISA-compatible with 8080
  • Direct addressing up to 1 MB
  • 16-bit arithmetic
