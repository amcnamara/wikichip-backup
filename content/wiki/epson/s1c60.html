S1C60 - Epson
< epson

Epson S1C60
Developer Epson
Manufacturer Epson
Type microcontrollers
Architecture 4-bit
ISA S1C60
Word size 4 bit
0.5 octets
1 nibbles
Technology CMOS
Clock 32.768 kHz-1 MHz
Package QFP13-64, QFP15-100, QFP14-80

The Epson S1C60 is a family of low-end 4-bit microcontrollers developed by Epson. This series are designed for very low power devices such as clocks and other appliances. This series of MCUs integrate a number of timers and an LCD driver. The S1C63 Family is a somewhat high-performance version of this family.
