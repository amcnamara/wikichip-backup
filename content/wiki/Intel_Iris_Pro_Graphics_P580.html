The Iris Pro P580 is a family of integrated graphics processor introduced by Intel for their Skylake-based microprocessors. These GPU include a large 128 MB of eDRAM.

This IGP supports DisplayPort, Embedded DisplayPort, HDMI, and DVI outputs. Supporting DirectX 12.1, OpenGL 4.4, OpenCL 2.0, HDMI 1.4, DP 1.2, and eDP 1.3.
