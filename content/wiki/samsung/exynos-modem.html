samsung/exynos-modem
< samsung

Samsung's Exynos Modems are a series of mobile radio modems under the Exynos brand.

Products[edit]

5G NR[edit]

Exynos Modem 5100 [1][edit]

  • 5G NR: Sub-6GHz, mmWave
  • Supported Modes: LTE-FDD, LTE-TDD, HSPA, TD-SCDMA, WCDMA, CDMA, GSM/EDGE
  • Downlink Features:
    • 8CA (Carrier Aggregation) in 5G NR
    • 8CA (Carrier Aggregation) in LTE
    • 4x4 MIMO
    • FD-MIMO
    • Up to 256-QAM in sub-6GHz
    • Up to 64-QAM in mmWave
  • Uplink Features:
    • 2CA (Carrier Aggregation) in 5G NR
    • 2CA (Carrier Aggregation) in LTE
    • Up to 256-QAM in sub-6GHz
    • Up to 64-QAM in mmWave
  • Process: 10nm FinFET Process

4G LTE[edit]

Exynos Modem 333 [2][edit]

  • LTE Cat. 10
  • Downlink: 3CA 450Mbps 64-QAM
  • Uplink: 2CA 100Mbps 16-QAM
  • 28nm HKMG Process

Exynos Modem 303 [3][edit]

  • LTE Cat. 6
  • Downlink: 2CA 300Mbps 64-QAM
  • Uplink: 100Mbps 16-QAM
  • 28nm HKMG Process

IoT[edit]

Exynos i T200 [4][edit]

  • CPU: Cortex®-R4 @ 320MHz, Cortex®-M0+ @ 320MHz
  • WiFi: 802.11b/g/n Single band (2.4GHz)
  • On-chip Memory: SRAM 1.4MB
  • Interface: SDIO/ I2C/ SPI/ UART/ PWM/ I2S
  • Front-end Module: Integrated T/R switch, Power Amplifier, Low Noise Amplifier
  • Security: WEP 64/128, WPA, WPA2, AES, TKIP, WAPI, PUF (Physically Unclonable Function)

Exynos i S111 [5][edit]

  • CPU: Cortex®-M7 200MHz
  • Modem: LTE Release 14 NB-IoT
    • Downlink: 127 kb/s
    • Uplink: 158 kb/s
  • On-chip Memory: SRAM 512KB
  • Interface: USI, UART, I2C, GPIO, eSIM I/F, SDIO(Host), QSPI(Single/Dual/Quad IO mode), SMC
  • Security: eFuse, AES, SHA-2, PKA, Secure Storage, Security Sub-System, PUF
  • GNSS: GPS, Galileo, GLONASS, BeiDou
