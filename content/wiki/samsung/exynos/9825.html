Exynos 9825 is a 64-bit octa-core ARM high performance mobile system on a chip designed by Samsung and introduced in mid-2019. The processor is fabricated on Samsung's 7nm EUV (Extreme Ultra Violet) FinFET process and features 8 cores in a tri-cluster configuration consisting of 2 Mongoose 4 big cores running at 2730 to 3016 MHz, 2 Cortex-A75 middle cores at 2400 MHz, and 4 Cortex-A55 little cores at 1950 to 2106 MHz. This chip supports up to 12 GiB of quad-channel 16-bit LPDDR4X-3600 memory and incorporates a Mali-G76 MP12 GPU. The 9825 incorporates an LTE modem supporting cat 20 download and upload.
